Town of Mount Crawford


Due to Waste Management no longer having an outlet for recyclables, recycling will terminate November 28th. You May keep your bins, take them to Mayor Cook's house or request pick up by emailing townofmountcrawford@gmail.com. Sorry for the short notice and any inconvenience. The Rockingham County landfill has recycling bins available for those who would like to recycle

Household Trash & Recycling Collection

  • All residents in Mt. Crawford are serviced by Mt. Crawford's contract with Waste Management.
  • Trash collection is weekly on Fridays
  • Recycling is collected on every other Wednesday.
    Items Eligible for Recycling
  • In inclement weather (snow, etc), trash or recycling will be collected the next working day

  • Billing for recycling and trash services are included in the monthly water/sewer bill. The current rate is $12.50 per month.

To request a Waste Management issued trash can or if you have any questions pertaining to trash and recycling collection, please email townofmountcrawford@gmail.com

Further Questions

If you have questions or concerns regarding Mt. Crawford Personal Trash Services, please email townofmountcrawford@gmail.com
