Town of Mount Crawford

Personal Property Taxes

  • Personal Property Taxes are paid to Mt. Crawford on an annual basis.
  • The Town's tax rate is $0.20 per $100 of assessed value. Assessments are determined by Rockingham County.
  • Every vehicle that has been registered to a Mt. Crawford resident and that has been regularly garaged, stored, or parked in the Town of Mt. Crawford anytime during the tax year is subject to this tax..
  • Personal Property Tax bills are due on January 31st and are typically mailed the month preceding the due date.

If you have questions or concerns regarding Mt. Crawford Personal Property Tax, please email townofmountcrawford@gmail.com
