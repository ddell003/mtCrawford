Town of Mount Crawford

Business License

  • A business license is required to operate a business in Mt. Crawford. Therefore, any person engaged in a business within the Town of Mt. Crawford must obtain a business license. This includes all phases of business, profession, trade, occupation or rentals conducted in the Town.
  • A flat fee of $25.00 is assessed for an annual license.
  • Business license applications are mailed to all existing businesses in the month preceding the renewal date of May 15th.

To obtain a new business license please email townofmountcrawford@gmail.com

Further Questions

If you have questions or concerns regarding Mt. Crawford business licenses, please email townofmountcrawford@gmail.com
